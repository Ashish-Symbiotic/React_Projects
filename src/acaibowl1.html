Miami Real Estate Advisor

David Rousso

David provides the clients he serves with an effortless experience from start to finish. He is known for diving deep into the market by understanding trends, architecture, interior design, and consumer needs. He has a unique talent in understanding the interior value of a home which stems from being a Co-Owner of Pedini Miami. Pedini Miami is a luxury design firm store for interior woodwork such Kitchen, Bathrooms, Closets & Special Furniture. David has experience working with high-profile individuals through the projects he has overseen at Pedini and uses those same skills in the world of Luxury Real Estate. David says, “As an advisor, I take pride in being more than a realtor by helping my clients with any of their home decisions”.

David has also developed a strong interest in new construction and real estate development by working daily with Builders, Investors, and End Users in the process of building a home. From finding the land to build on to connecting them with the architects and designers, David is a one-stop agent that can connect several parts to bring a deal together.

Originally from Caracas, Venezuela, David has grown up in Miami since the age of 5. He is fluent in English and Spanish. After attending high school at Doral Academy, he spent 4 years obtaining his Business Degree at Florida International University. David plans to continue his studies in order to continue to learn and adapt to better serve his clients day in and day out.
